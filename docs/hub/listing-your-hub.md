A new hub is empty until people can find it. Public hublists are the main way users discover hubs, so listing yours is how you get visitors.

## How hublists work

A hublist is a directory that DC clients load. Your hub gets onto a list either by registering it with the hublist, or by having your hub announce itself to the list (many hub programs can ping a hublist automatically). Once listed, your hub shows up with its name, description, address, user count, and any share requirement.

To list your hub, pick one or more hublists (such as [DCHublist](https://dchublist.org/)), follow their submission or registration steps, and make sure your hub is reachable so the list can verify it.
