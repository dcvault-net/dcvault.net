This guide gets you from nothing to your first hub in a few steps.

## 1. Pick a client

Choose a Direct Connect client for your platform. If you are unsure, start with DC++ on Windows or AirDC++ on Windows or Linux. See [Choosing a client](/docs/clients/choosing-a-client) and the [client list](/docs/clients/dc-clients).

## 2. Install and set it up

Install the client and work through the first-run settings: your nickname, connection, downloads, and your share. The [Installation & setup](/docs/clients/installation-setup) walkthrough covers each screen.

## 3. Get active mode working

Open the right ports on your router so other clients can reach you. This makes search and downloads work properly. See [Active vs. passive mode](/docs/clients/active-vs-passive-mode).

## 4. Join a hub

Open the public hub list, pick a hub, and connect. Check its share rules first. See [Finding & joining hubs](/docs/clients/finding-joining-hubs).

## 5. Search and download

Browse a user's file list or search the hub, then download straight from other users. See [Searching & downloading](/docs/clients/searching-downloading).

:::tip[Take your time]
Everyone needs a little time to get used to DC. If something is unclear, ask on the [forum](https://forum.dcvault.net).
:::
