Hubs are where people meet. Once your client is set up, you connect to a hub to chat, search, and download.

## Connecting in DC++

![DC++ public hub list](/img/getting-started/6_connect.png)

1. Open the list of public hubs. The lists are fetched and updated automatically.
2. Pick a hub that appeals to you. Check its share rules first, some hubs require a minimum share size.
3. Right-click a hub to connect directly or add it to your favorites.

:::info[Be a good guest]
Follow the hub's rules, be friendly, and do not spam.
:::

Once you are on a hub, move on to [searching & downloading](/docs/clients/searching-downloading).
